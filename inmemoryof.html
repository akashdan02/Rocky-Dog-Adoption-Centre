This organization was started in the memory of Rocky and is named after Rocky. Rocky was a very kind, sweet, benevolent, lovable and friendly dog. Rocky was born on April 14,2010. Due to blood clots inside his stomach, Rocky started becoming weaker day by day and was vomiting every food he consumed and eventually Rocky passed away on January 3,2017. Although Rocky has left us physically, Rocky still lives in our hearts and will never be forgotten.
