"WE ARE HERE FOR THE WELFARE OF ABANDONED DOGS"

We are open 24/7 working for the welfare of any animals but primarily for dogs as they're the only pets which gets abandoned more often. These innocent animals have a real rough and hard life to live and they aren't even able to communicate and say their pain to anyone. Animals have souls just like us human beings. Some humans throw rocks on dogs for no actual reason and hurt them. Such activities aren't tolerated. So, we are a group of friends who started this organization to rescue the stranded animals and provide medication, shelter and food until we find a new home for them where they are loved and treated equal
